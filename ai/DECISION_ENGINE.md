
# Financial Decision Engine

Purpose:
Allow AI to compare financial decisions.

Directory:
backend/app/decision/

User question:

"Should I buy a car?"

Engine runs:

Scenario A – Buy car
Scenario B – No purchase
Scenario C – Buy cheaper car

Output:

| Scenario | Debt Free | Savings | Risk |
|--------|--------|--------|------|
Buy Car | 2030 | 120M | Medium |
No Car | 2028 | 210M | Low |

AI uses results to generate recommendation.
